Some time later...

After all the interviews, celebrations and congratulations after the match, your body has finally had enough.
Your sore muscles ache, your vision is blurry and all the endorphins from your big victory are used up.
After making up excuses to your team (who are too busy celebrating to notice anyway), you sneak out of the building
to start your walk home. You think about getting a taxi for a moment, but your apartment is within walking distance.
It is already dark outside and the fresh air helps to clear your head. Just as you start enjoying your little walk, you here a voice:

"No Way!" Is that who I think it is?"

Your turn around, only to find three young adults staring at you with a mix off awe and disbelief.
By the merch they're wearing, you deduce that they are probably fans of yours.

How do you react?
